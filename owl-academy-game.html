Olwen's Workshop

"Three charms, apprentice." She presses them into your hand. "Walk up to whatever's in your way out there and try one. You'll feel it when it's right. Now go — the road will teach you faster than I can."

How to Play

🎯
The actual goal — reach the Ancient Ruins door (southeast, past the Castle), open it with Memory Pulse, go inside, and seal all three rifts you find there while dodging the Thief's attacks. That's how the game ends. The Goal bar under the top HUD always tells you exactly how many tiles away it is.
🥇
Spotting the door — every blocked obstacle pulses white except the Ruins door, which pulses GOLD.
🔮
Shards are optional — currency for Owl Scouts and upgrades. You can't get permanently stuck without them: the basic shards never require Owl Scouts to reach, and the final room always has a couple of its own.
🕹️
Move — hold the arrow pad (or arrow keys / WASD). Talk — walk up to a character and tap ✋.
🪄
The three charms (one idea, three forms):
Memory Pulse — already known, free, instant. Can go stale.
Sky Scan — true right now, costs ⚡. Fixes staleness.
Owl Scouts — somewhere you can't reach, costs 🔮. About reach, not freshness.
